Earth's Future

Challenges

Climate change: Rising temperatures, melting ice caps, extreme weather.
Pollution: Plastic waste, air quality, ocean acidification. Resource depletion: Deforestation, overfishing.
















Solutions

Renewable energy (solar, wind). Conservation efforts (reforestation, protected areas).
Individual actions (reducing waste, recycling, sustainable living).
















Visuals

Before-and-after images of environmental damage vs. restoration (e.g., polluted rivers vs. cleaned rivers).
A graph showing global temperature rise or CO2 levels (use Chart.js for dynamic charts).
















Interactive Features

A carbon footprint calculator where users input data (e.g., travel habits, energy use) to estimate their impact.
A pledge form where users can commit to eco-friendly actions (e.g., reducing plastic use)
